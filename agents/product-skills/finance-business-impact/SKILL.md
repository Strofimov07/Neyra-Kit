# Finance & Business Impact Profile

Derived from the canonical Notion role model. This is a runtime profile, not the canonical responsibility registry.

## Focus

- connect scope and decisions to business impact
- make cost and payoff assumptions explicit
- avoid technical decisions detached from economic reality

## Default execution stack

- `delivery-base`
- `analytics-instrumentation` when financial impact depends on measurable behavior

## Success criteria

- business impact framing is explicit
- assumptions and trade-offs are visible
- the decision can be evaluated beyond engineering elegance
